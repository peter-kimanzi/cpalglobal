10. Certificate in Environmental Health Sciences

The Certificate in Environmental Health Science is a Public Health Technician Course. It is a programme that trains you on how to identify and analyse environments and hazardous agents. At the end of your training, you will have enough knowledge on how to limit exposures to toxic biological, chemical, and physical agents in elements that affect people’s health.
REQUIREMENTS

KSCE mean grade of C-

C- in English or Kiswahili

D+ in Biology or Biological Sciences

D (plain) in Mathematics

D (plain) in Agriculture, Building Construction, Chemistry, Electricity and Wood Work, Home Science, Physics, and Technical Drawing

11. Certificate in Health Records and Information Technology

If you are a fresh high school graduate, you can enroll for a Certificate in Health Records and Information Technology. It is a course that prepares you to become a medical record and health information technician responsible for ensuring the safety of patient’s records and keeping them updated.
REQUIREMENTS

KSCE mean grade of C-

C- in English or Kiswahili

D in Biology or Biological Sciences

D- in Mathematics

D+ in any one of the following subjects at KCSE level: Agriculture, Chemistry, Commerce and Business Studies, Computer Studies, Economics, General Science, Geography, Home Science, and Physics, Physical Science.
